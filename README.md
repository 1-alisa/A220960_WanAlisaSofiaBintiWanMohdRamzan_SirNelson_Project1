Here is a simplified version of your **Problem Statement** and **Objectives**, focusing on the most important points for your SlideGenie app.

### **Problem Statement**
Students today face significant challenges in managing their academic life due to disorganized schedules and scattered study materials. Without a centralized system to track classes and store information, students often experience unnecessary stress and a decrease in learning efficiency, which hinders the goal of achieving **SDG 4: Quality Education**.

**Key Issues:**
*   **Time Management:** Difficulty tracking rotating class schedules leads to missed lessons or confusion.
*   **Information Fragmentation:** Study notes are often stored in different places, making it hard to retrieve info quickly.
*   **Academic Stress:** Poor organization creates a heavy mental burden that affects overall student well-being.

---

### **Project Objectives**
The SlideGenie app aims to transform the student experience by digitizing academic organization into one cohesive platform. The primary focus is to remove the barriers that prevent students from focusing on their actual studies.

**Core Goals:**
*   **Unified Timetable:** Create a clear, color-coded digital schedule to help students visualize their weekly commitments.
*   **Efficient Note-Taking:** Provide a dedicated space for quick notes to ensure all academic information is kept in one secure app.
*   **Stress Reduction:** Simplify the daily routine of a student to improve focus and academic performance.
*   **Accessible Productivity:** Offer a user-friendly tool that supports digital literacy and quality education for all users.
