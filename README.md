# SlideGenie SDG4 (WAN ALISA SOFIA BINTI WAN MOHD RAMZAN A220960)

## Problem Statement

Students often struggle to manage their academic life because schedules and study materials are not organized in one place. This can cause stress, confusion, and lower learning efficiency, which affects the achievement of **SDG 4: Quality Education**.

### Key Issues

* Difficulty managing class schedules and tasks
* Notes and study materials are scattered in different places
* Poor organization increases academic stress and reduces productivity

---

## Project Objectives

SlideGenie is designed to help students manage their studies more easily through one mobile application.

### Goals

* Provide a digital timetable for better schedule management
* Store notes and study materials in one place
* Reduce academic stress through better organization
* Improve productivity with a simple and user-friendly app

---

## Proposed Solution

SlideGenie is a student productivity mobile application that combines academic tools into one platform.

### Features

* **Smart Timetable** – Organize schedules, reminders, and subjects
* **Note Storage** – Save and manage study notes securely
* **Task & Quiz Management** – Track tasks, quizzes, and study progress
* **Study Focus Tools** – Include study timers and break reminders
* **User-Friendly Interface** – Simple design for easy navigation

---

## Expected Impact

SlideGenie is expected to:

* Improve students’ time management and organization
* Reduce stress caused by poor scheduling
* Increase productivity and learning efficiency
* Support students’ academic performance and well-being
* Contribute to **SDG 4: Quality Education** through accessible digital learning tools
