# SlideGenie

## Problem Statement

Students today face significant challenges in managing their academic life due to disorganized schedules and scattered study materials. Without a centralized system to track classes and store information, students often experience unnecessary stress and a decrease in learning efficiency, which hinders the achievement of **SDG 4: Quality Education**.

### Key Issues

* **Time Management** – Difficulty tracking rotating class schedules often leads to missed lessons, forgotten tasks, and confusion.
* **Information Fragmentation** – Study notes and academic materials are stored in different places, making information difficult to access quickly.
* **Academic Stress** – Poor organization increases mental burden and negatively affects students’ focus, productivity, and well-being.

---

## Project Objectives

The SlideGenie app aims to improve the student learning experience by digitizing academic organization into one integrated platform. The project focuses on helping students manage their studies more effectively while reducing stress and improving productivity.

### Core Goals

* **Unified Timetable** – Provide a clear, color-coded digital schedule to help students manage classes and academic activities efficiently.
* **Efficient Note-Taking** – Create a dedicated and secure space for storing notes and study materials in one application.
* **Stress Reduction** – Simplify daily academic routines to improve concentration and academic performance.
* **Accessible Productivity** – Deliver a user-friendly application that supports digital learning and quality education for all students.

---

## Proposed Solution

To solve the issues of disorganized schedules, scattered study materials, and academic stress, the proposed solution is the development of SlideGenie, a mobile productivity application specially designed for students.

SlideGenie acts as a centralized academic management system where students can organize schedules, notes, quizzes, and study activities in one platform. By integrating productivity and learning tools into a single mobile application, SlideGenie helps students improve learning efficiency and maintain better academic organization.

### Solution Features

#### Smart Timetable Management

Students can create and manage digital timetables with organized schedules, reminders, and color-coded subjects. This feature improves time management and reduces the risk of missing classes or important tasks.

#### Centralized Note Storage

The app provides a note-taking feature where students can securely store and manage all academic notes in one place. This allows faster access to important information and reduces information fragmentation.

#### Task and Quiz Management

Students can create daily tasks, quizzes, and study checklists to monitor academic progress and improve productivity.

#### Study Focus Tools

SlideGenie includes study timers and break reminders to encourage better concentration, healthy study habits, and balanced learning routines.

#### User-Friendly Interface

The application is designed with a simple and accessible interface, allowing students to navigate the app easily regardless of their technical experience.

---

## Expected Impact

The implementation of SlideGenie is expected to:

* Improve students’ time management and academic organization.
* Reduce stress caused by poor scheduling and scattered information.
* Enhance productivity and learning efficiency through centralized academic tools.
* Encourage digital literacy and self-management skills among students.
* Support better academic performance and overall student well-being.
* Contribute to the achievement of **SDG 4: Quality Education** by providing accessible educational productivity tools for students.
